  1. Are they indestructible?

    2. Overwatering, poor drainage and the use of the wrong compost are easy to destroy a cactus plant. The usual potting compost retains water well which is news for the most plant but not for the cactus plant as a cactus plant need good drainage. To this, the cactus plant needs the use of specialist compost to survive.



  1. Do they flower?

    2. Cactus plants are all flowering plants; however, some have more prominent blooms than others. Mammillaria, Parodia and Gymnocalycium flower easily with an impressive and colourful display.

  2. How do I get them to flower?

    3. It is very unlikely of the cactus plant to sit there not flowering. It is important to get the cactus plant to follow its natural growth cycle. In winter, it needs to go dormant hence it requires to be kept in a dry and cold but not dark area, and stop watering.
